GURUDEV PUBLIC SCHOOL BALBADDA is a Co-education, English Medium school affiliated to JAC, State Board of JHARKHAND.GURUDEV PUBLIC SCHOOL BALBADDA was started in the year 2008 at BALBADDA to meet the educational needs of the growing villages. The school belongs to Mr. ARUN KUMAR SINHA. However the new campus at BALBADDA in GAJANDHA road started in the year 2016.GURUDEV PUBLIC SCHOOL BALBADDA provides a congenial and aspiring environment for all round development of personality.
